Concept Statement

Our project is a Career Development Resources website that specializes in connecting undergraduate students interested in tech careers with the resources needed for success. The application will be designed to help our users maximize the exposure benefits of tech-related resources that are accessible, usable, and functional. We will create a cutting-edge website that offers resources for internships and entry level positions to help students with their career hunting process. Our website will offer efficient navigation for resources related to the job hunting process, from finding opportunities to preparing and acing technical/behavioral interviews.
